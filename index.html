Conflict Resolution

In our community, we explore ethical non-monogamous relationships. While there are many books on this topic, every one of us has a different approach to our relationships. Even with the best of intentions, any of us could end up behaving in uninformed or insensitive ways, or committing micro-aggressions: We can all mess up! How do we deal with that?
